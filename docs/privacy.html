Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Short version: crates does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server. Everything stays on your device.

What Data crates Accesses

crates connects to your TIDAL account using OAuth 2.0 to read the following information:

  • Your saved album library (album names, artists, artwork, release dates, audio quality tiers)
  • Your TIDAL display name (shown in the app settings)
  • Track listings for albums you open (fetched on demand)

This data is requested using the user.read and collection.read scopes only. crates does not request access to your listening history, playlists, personal messages, or any other TIDAL data.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data — including your album library, crate organization, and TIDAL credentials — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. Nothing is sent to any crates server, because there is no crates server.

Your TIDAL access tokens are stored in your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and are never logged or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

TIDAL: crates communicates directly with the TIDAL API to sync your album library and fetch track listings. Your interaction with TIDAL is governed by TIDAL's Privacy Policy.

MusicBrainz: crates queries the MusicBrainz open database to enrich album genre metadata. Album identifiers (artist name, album name) are sent to MusicBrainz to retrieve genre information. MusicBrainz is a non-profit open music encyclopedia. No personal information is shared. Their privacy policy is available at metabrainz.org/privacy.

crates does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools that collect personal data.

Data Sharing

crates does not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. There is no crates account, no user database, and no backend infrastructure that holds your information.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling crates removes all locally stored data from your device. To revoke crates' access to your TIDAL account at any time, visit your TIDAL account settings and remove crates from the list of connected applications.

Children's Privacy

crates is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
