What crates Does

A collector's tool.
Not another streaming UI.

Signature Feature

Browse by Audio Quality

TIDAL tells you the quality tier of every album in your collection — Hi-Res Lossless, Lossless, High, Low. crates surfaces this as a first-class view mode. Know exactly what you own at the highest fidelity.

Tagging

Tag Your Albums, Your Way

Create your own tags — Late Night, Road Trip, Jazz Fusion, anything. Apply multiple tags to any album with a long press. Browse your entire library by tag, and see at a glance which albums are tagged from the collection view.

Collection

Your Full Library, Instantly

crates opens to your full TIDAL album collection. Browse by artist, genre, decade, quality, recently added, or your own tags. Every view mode is one tap away from the top of the screen.

Crates

Organize Into Named Crates

Create crates for any intent — Jazz Fusion, Late Night, Road Trip, MQA Showcase. Add albums with a single tap. Albums can live in multiple crates simultaneously.

Search

Search and Add to Your Collection

Search the full TIDAL catalog by title or artist directly inside crates. Preview artwork, creator, and release year before adding. Albums land in your TIDAL collection and sync back to crates automatically.

Discovery

Random Album Picker

Can't decide what to play? Hit the dice. crates picks a random album from your collection and opens it ready to play. The best albums are the ones you forgot you had.

Discovery

Long Press to Peek Inside a Crate

Long press any crate to reveal a random shuffle of album art from inside. A living, tactile reminder of what's waiting — rediscovery built into the gesture.

Playback

Play Instantly in TIDAL

Tap any album to open it directly in the official TIDAL app. crates hands off via deep link and gets out of the way. One tap, always.

Metadata

Real Genre Data

TIDAL's genre data is incomplete. crates enriches your library automatically using Last.fm — so Genre sorting actually reflects what you own.

Collection

Remove Albums from Your Collection

Long press any album and open its detail view to remove it from your TIDAL collection directly. A confirmation step keeps accidental taps from doing damage.

Search

Full Artist Discography

Search by artist and browse their complete catalog — not just recent releases. Paginated across TIDAL's full archive. Toggle between newest-first and oldest-first to dig through a back catalog in order.

Privacy

No Account. No Server.

Your library data, crates, and tags live in a local database on your device. Nothing leaves your phone. There is no crates cloud.
